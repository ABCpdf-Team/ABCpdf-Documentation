ReduceSizeOperation Class    

Operation to reduce the size of a document.

The default settings here are aimed a reasonably aggressive size reduction. The most common changes you may wish to make relate to monochrome (eg black and white) images and palettization. The default settings here may be too aggressive for some applications so may require a little moderation. See the MonochromeImageDpi property for details.
